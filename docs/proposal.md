
# Project Name: AI Quiz Generator

## Problem Statement:
Students often struggle to turn their notes into practice questions/quizzes to better comprehend the material, creating quizzes manually can be time consuming which leads to poor preparation and lower academic performance. Our AI tool helps students create fast and effecient quizzes from their notes or lectures slides which increases their retention of knowledge on the course materials. 


## Architecture
![Architecture Diagram](UGR 277-Capstone Project-Quiz Generator Architecture.drawio (1).png)  

## Components
*Data Ingestion: Upload notes using Airtable form and send data to AI.
*AI Processing: Flowise AI generates quiz questions from notes.
*0utput: Quiz is delivered to user and results shown in dashboard.


## How to Run
1. Upload notes using Airtable form
2. System generates quiz questions automatically
3. User completes quiz and views results
